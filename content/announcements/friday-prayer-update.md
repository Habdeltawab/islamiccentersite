Dear Community Members,

Starting March 2026, we are adjusting our Friday Jummah prayer schedule to better serve our community.

## New Schedule

| Service | Time |
|---------|------|
| First Khutbah | 12:30 PM |
| First Prayer | 1:00 PM |
| Second Khutbah | 1:30 PM |
| Second Prayer | 2:00 PM |

## Important Notes

- Please arrive at least 15 minutes early
- Parking is limited, consider carpooling
- Children's program available during prayer
- Sisters' section is on the upper level

## Why Two Jummah Services?

Due to our growing community, we have added a second Jummah service to accommodate everyone comfortably. Both services will have the same khutbah content.

## Parking Guidelines

1. Use the main parking lot first
2. Overflow parking available on the street
3. Please do not block driveways
4. Carpool when possible

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.
