Assalamu Alaikum dear community members,

You are cordially invited to our monthly community potluck dinner!

## Event Details

- **Date**: Saturday, March 21, 2026
- **Time**: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
- **Location**: Islamic Center of Ankeny - Main Hall
- **Maghrib Prayer**: Will be prayed in congregation

## What to Bring

Please bring a dish to share that serves 8-10 people. Some suggestions:

- Main dishes (rice, meat, pasta)
- Salads and sides
- Desserts
- Beverages (non-alcoholic)

**Important**: Please label your dish if it contains common allergens (nuts, dairy, gluten).

## Program

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 6:00 PM | Arrival & Setup |
| 6:30 PM | Maghrib Prayer |
| 7:00 PM | Dinner |
| 8:00 PM | Short Talk |
| 8:30 PM | Isha Prayer |
| 9:00 PM | Cleanup & Departure |

## For Kids

- Supervised play area available
- Games and activities planned
- Please bring indoor shoes for children

While RSVP is not required, it helps us plan better. Please let us know if you're coming:

We look forward to seeing you there!
