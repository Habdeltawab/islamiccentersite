Assalamu Alaikum,

We are pleased to announce that registration for our Spring 2026 Quran classes is now open!

## Classes Offered

### Children's Program (Ages 5-15)

- **Schedule**: Saturdays & Sundays, 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- **Curriculum includes**:
  - Quran reading with Tajweed
  - Memorization (Hifz) track available
  - Islamic Studies
  - Arabic basics

### Adult Program

- **Schedule**: Tuesday & Thursday, 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM
- **Levels**:
  - Beginner (learning Arabic letters)
  - Intermediate (reading practice)
  - Advanced (Tajweed perfection)

## Fees
